10. Q.567+56–4!nt: ✨Sebenarnya aku punya bakat terpendam, tapi aku lupa di pendam di mana✨​


567 + 56 - 4!

= 567 + 56 - (4 × 3 × 2 × 1)

= 567 + 56 - (12 × 2 × 1)

= 567 + 56 - (24 × 1)

= 567 + 56 - 24

= 623 - 24

= 599

________________________________

JAWABAN :

567 + 56 - 4! =

= 567 + 56 - 4!

= 567 + 56 - ( 4 × 3 × 2 × 1 )

= 567 + 56 - ( 12 × 2 × 1 )

= 567 + 56 - ( 24 × 1 )

= 567 + 56 - 24

= 623 - 24

= 599

Jadi, hasil dari 567 + 56 - 4! adalah 599
